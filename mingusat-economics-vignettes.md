# Mingusat Economics Vignettes



## Commodity Flow: A Typical "Cocoa Run"
A closer look at a single 500-ton haul:
1.  **Sangay (Farm Gate)**:
    *   Load: 300 tons Green Coffee / Raw Cocoa.
    *   *Cost*: Equity transfer to Agricultural Guild.
2.  **Transit (14 Days)**:
    *   *Action*: Hulling and drying machinery runs off ship's reactor.
    *   *Value Add*: "Space-Cured" label applied. Value +40%.
3.  **Samūm (The Combs)**:
    *   Offload: Processed Cocoa/Coffee.
    *   Load: 50 tons **Precision Optics**, 20 tons **Poatag Leather Rolls**, 100 crates **Industrial Drones**.
4.  **Ísreikistjarna (The Elevator)**:
    *   Offload: Drones & Optics (for the Elevator maintenance).
    *   Load: 200 tons **Compressed Ice** (Ballast/Commodity), 10 tons **Regenerative Serums**.
5.  **Return to Sangay**:
    *   The ice is sold to Nüüdelchid processing stations en route; Serums delivered to Sangay clinics.
